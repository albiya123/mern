pallikunnu, located in Idukki district of Kerala, is a picturesque hill station known for its breathtaking panoramic views and serene ambiance. The name "Parunthumpara" translates to "Eagle Rock," inspired by the bird-like shape of the rocky hilltop or the eagle's eye view the location offers. Surrounded by lush greenery, rolling hills, and deep valleys, it is a popular spot for nature lovers, trekkers, and photographers. Visitors often flock here to enjoy the misty mornings, mesmerizing sunsets, and the enchanting view of the Sabarimala forests, especially during the pilgrimage season.
